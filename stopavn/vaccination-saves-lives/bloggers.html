Bloggers: we need your help!

There are a few ways you can help support the Vaccination Saves Lives campaign

Write about the campaign!

This is the most important way you can help! Tell your readers about issues related to the AVN, vaccination science or policy! Don't forget to mention us and link to http://www.stopavn.com/vaccination-saves-lives, where you can also get some more information about the campaign. If you need more ideas (we have lots!), come and talk to us on Facebook at facebook.com/stopavn

Add a badge to your sidebar

You can help us spread the word about the life-saving benefits of vaccination, and the misinformation of the AVN, by adding our badge to your sidebar. Here's how it looks:

To add this to your site, copy the following code and paste it into your site's code where you would like it to appear:


<div style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.stopavn.com/vaccination-saves-lives" title="Vaccination Saves Lives: Stop The Australian Vaccination Network"><img src="http://www.stopavn.com/images/vaccination-saves-lives-badge.png" width="180" height="100" title="Vaccination Saves Lives: Stop The Australian Vaccination Network" alt="Vaccination Saves Lives: Stop The Australian Vaccination Network" /></a></div>

If you use WordPress, you can easily add this button to your sidebar by pasting the code above into a Text Widget.

If you use Blogger, you can include the button by pasting the code into a “HTML/JavaScript” Gadget from the design window of your blog.

Add a banner to posts about the AVN

If you are writing about topics related to the AVN, Meryl Dorey, Woodford Folk Festival, we'd love it if you could add our banner to the top or bottom of your post!

If you're writing about Stop the AVN, our Vaccinations Save Lives Campaign or our plane-towed banner at Woodford, then you should use our banner, too!

Here's how it looks:

And here's the code to get it:
